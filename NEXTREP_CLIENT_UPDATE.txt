NextRep — Build Update
======================

TL;DR
-----
The full team/individual marketplace experience from the spec is built and tested
end-to-end. The only things not done are the two items the spec already flagged as
"future" (donations) or "to be determined" (returns). Everything is on a review
branch, ready to merge.


WHAT'S WORKING NOW
------------------
- Account types at sign-up — new users choose "Team account" or "Individual" up
  front, and land in the right experience.
- Teams & codes — a team gets a unique, non-expiring join code it can copy and
  reshare.
- Joining teams — individuals join by entering a code (verified against real
  teams), can belong to multiple teams, and add codes anytime.
- Gear listings — gear posted by a team or its members is automatically linked to
  the team.
- Team dashboard ("virtual warehouse") — member roster, all team + member gear,
  # listed, # sold, and revenue. Public vs. private figures handled per the spec.
- Individual dashboard — your gear, items sold/purchased, revenue, your teams, and
  your sports.
- Get paid — sellers can list before connecting Stripe and are prompted to connect
  to receive payouts.
- Polish — team names shown (not just codes), dashboard links in the menu,
  clickable rosters/listings, and copy in English/German/Spanish/French.

Everything above is covered by an automated test suite (1,200+ checks passing).


INTENTIONALLY NOT BUILT YET (per the spec)

- Donations (% of a sale to a team) — marked "future"; it changes the payment
  flow, so we're holding for your go-ahead.
- Returns / disputes — "to be determined" in the spec; needs requirements before
  we build.
